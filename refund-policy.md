<div class="policy-page">
  <h1>Refund &amp; Cancellation Policy</h1>
  <p class="policy-intro">Last updated: May 2026</p>

  <div class="policy-section">
    <h2>1. Booking Confirmation</h2>
    <p>Once a registration is confirmed and payment is received, it is considered final. Please review all event details carefully before completing your registration.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="policy-section">
    <h2>2. No-Refund Policy</h2>
    <p>All event registrations are <strong>non-refundable</strong>. Cancellations are not accepted once a registration has been completed. We encourage participants to ensure they are available before registering.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="policy-section">
    <h2>3. Event Cancellation by Bangalore Chess Club</h2>
    <p>In the rare event that Bangalore Chess Club cancels an event:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Registered participants will be notified promptly via email and/or phone.</li>
      <li>A <strong>full refund</strong> will be issued to the original payment method.</li>
      <li>Refunds will be processed within <strong>5–7 working days</strong>.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="policy-section">
    <h2>4. Event Rescheduling</h2>
    <p>If an event is rescheduled, your registration will automatically carry over to the new date. If you are unable to attend the rescheduled event, please contact us and we will evaluate the situation on a case-by-case basis.</p>
  </div>
