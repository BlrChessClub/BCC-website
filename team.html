Rook and Roll

Bangalore chess club organized a Simul event featuring IM Rakesh Kulkarni a day before the FIDE World Championship 2023. Out of 20 players, Gurudeep Mathad and Nazeef Khan secured victories against the IM, while Vrajesh, Samaksh Ashok, and Rahil Shah managed draws, with the IM winning the Simul by 16.5-3.5.

World Chess Day

World Chess Day, established by UNESCO in 1966 and celebrated on July 20, promotes global chess growth. In Bengaluru, increased participation during the pandemic showcases rising interest, especially among children, with preparations for the Chess Olympiad in focus..
